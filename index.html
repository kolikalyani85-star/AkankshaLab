Meet Our Directors

The visionary leadership guiding Akanksha Analytical towards excellence since 2006.

Partner
Mr. Mahendra Katariya
Partner & Strategic Director — 30+ Years of Leadership
Mr. Mahendra Katariya brings over three decades of leadership experience in environmental compliance and laboratory operations. He is instrumental in steering the organization with a clear focus on regulatory excellence, operational integrity, and client trust.

With a strong foundation in commerce (B.Com.) and deep domain expertise, Mr. Katariya is widely recognized for his proficiency in State Pollution Control Board (SPCB) compliance. He has consistently guided industries in meeting stringent environmental regulations, ensuring seamless alignment with statutory requirements and sustainable practices.

Under his leadership, the laboratory has achieved and maintained key recognitions from MoEF, NABL, and adherence to ISO standards. He has successfully led and supported critical environmental infrastructure projects, including ETP, STP, and RO systems.

Driven by a commitment to environmental protection, quality, and sustainability, he continues to strengthen the laboratory's reputation as a trusted partner for industries and regulators alike. Established as a recognized MoEF laboratory since 2006, the organization reflects his enduring vision of delivering reliable, compliant, and future-ready environmental solutions.

B.Com. SPCB Compliance Expert ETP / STP / RO Systems Regulatory Advisory

Ms. Vaishali Katariya is a key pillar of the organization, bringing over 20 years of experience in administration, accounts, and operational management. With a strong commitment to quality and sustainability, Ms. Katariya contributes significantly to maintaining the laboratory's credibility and operational excellence.
